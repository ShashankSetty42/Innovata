Rules and Regulations

  1. Candidates must belong to B.Tech /BE course of any Engineering College in India/IIT/NIT/Universities.
  2. All the members of the team should be a student of a Registered Educational Institute and be graduating in 2017 or later.
  3. No postgraduate students (M.E/M.Tech/M.Sc/Doctorate) are allowed.
  4. One project can be registered in any one stream irrespective of the branch of study of project team members.
  5. Teams can choose any discipline according to their project.
  6. One Project can be registered under only one discipline and theme competition
    • LabVIEW
    • Civil
    • Computer Science
    • Electrical
    • Electronics
    • Instrumentation
    • Mechanical
  7. Besides, each team can opt for Theme based competition also.
  8. Maximum number of students allowed for a team is 5.
  9. General / Case Study projects will not be considered. 
  10. Teams are not allowed to change the team members once the registration process is completed.
  11. Teams can show their results in the form of 3D models,drawings, posters, computer generated(simulation) models and working models.
